Work at Sawhorse Media

Sawhorse is a pretty awesome place to work. Some of the bigger benefits are:

  • Work on multiple, interesting projects.
  • Sawhorse is funded by our customers, not venture capitalists.
  • Big decisions are made by our team, not a board of directors.
  • Drawings on desks is highly encouraged.
  • We run a real business that makes money.

We have multiple products that reach an audience of millions. We want you to feel ownership and excitement about what you’re building, and as a part of a small startup you’ll have a lot of influence to shape the company and engineering direction overall.

These positions are based primarily out of our SoHo office, but with the added flexibility of working from home, a café, or wherever you're at your best.

Our products:

Muck Rack

Muck Rack is a series of tools to filter and analyze how journalists are covering the news in real-time, alongside Muck Rack Pro helping you find out what journalists are saying about things that matter to your business or organization.

Shorty Awards

The Shorty Awards honor the best of social media each year. Millions of people participate in The Shortys to recognize individuals and organizations producing great content on Twitter, Facebook, Tumblr, YouTube, Foursquare and the rest of the social web.

Our tech stack

Python, Django, Celery, Sass, Redis, Memcached, Solr, Nginx, Gunicorn, Varnish, Ansible, Github, Linode
