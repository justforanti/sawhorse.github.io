Software Engineer - NY

Build features and infrastructure for Muck Rack. An ideal candidate understands what makes great (and maintainable) code, as well as a feel for what makes for good user interactions. Experience with software-as-a-service sites or working at a startup is a plus.

Requirements

  • Passionate about writing great code and an adherent to the Zen of Python
  • Facility with Javascript and CSS, and the issues in writing clear, maintainable and testable front-end code
  • Can write readable, testable, quality code
  • Experience working in a fast paced development environment with testing, continuous integration and multiple daily deployments.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work closely with designers and product managers
  • Flexibility, great problem-solving skills, and sense of humor

Preferred

  • Familiarity with managing servers especially with tools such as Ansible
  • Interested in journalism, news, media or social media
  • Worked at or founded a startup
  • Skilled at moving Trello cards from the left to the right.

How to apply

First, create your profile on Muck Rack. Second, send us an email at jobs@sawhorsemedia.com and:

  • Include links that demonstrate your work, such as your Github profile, personal site, or live projects you’ve built or worked on
  • Tell us why you’re interested in working at Sawhorse
  • Attachments should be PDF, not MS Word.
