Freelance Designer

We're looking for a freelance designer to help us design beautiful yet functional interfaces, as well as take care of various projects that come up.

We're looking for someone who can:

  • Design and code interfaces for new features, or iterations on existing features
  • Ideate, sketch wireframes and implement those designs
  • You believe in clean, effective, scalable and modular design/development
  • You know that designing products is way more than just "pretty"
  • Redesign email templates (responsive, outlook friendly, etc)
  • Design marketing collateral
  • Hand-codes html, css (.sass)
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work closely with designers and product managers
  • Highly honed ability to use emoticons and reaction .gifs in inter-office chat
  • Flexibility, great problem-solving skills, and sense of humor

Preferred

  • Django templating is a plus
  • Experience with UX / researching is a plus

How to apply

First, create your profile on Muck Rack. Second, send us an email at jobs@sawhorsemedia.com and:

  • Include links that demonstrate your work, such as your Github profile, personal site, or live projects you’ve built or worked on
  • Tell us why you’re interested in working at Sawhorse
  • Attachments should be PDF, not MS Word.
