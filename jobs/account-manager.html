Customer Success Manager

Muck Rack is looking for an ambitious individual who is looking to join one of the fastest growing tech startups in NYC. Muck Rack's mission is to make journalists, PR pros and marketers more successful by providing the technology to do so. This includes helping media professionals find journalists to pitch and monitor on a day to day basis via Muck Rack Pro, our advanced service. You’ll be working alongside the CEO, SVP & Senior Account Strategists to maintain accounts, provide support and increase inside sales and retention. This is a full time job based in our NYC headquarters.

Responsibilities include:

  • Managing the needs of our journalists and customers and making sure they’re successful
  • Identify processes to improve to make our customers happy and implementing improvements.
  • Solving customer inquiries and communicating through a CRM tool, email, Twitter or phone.
  • Organizing, implementing and managing trials
  • Tracking feedback from customers and users
  • Providing client support for technical issues via email, phone, and Web conferencing tools.
  • Acting as a liaison between clients and the product development team
  • Managing inbound leads, demoing the tools and shareable materials
  • Setting up calls and meetings with decision makers
  • Tracking feedback from customers and users
  • Contributing to and monitoring Muck Rack’s editorial channels
  • Becoming involved in the public relations and journalism industry in New York and beyond
  • Hitting retention goals and taking on new projects when needed
  • Demoing Muck Rack at meetings, on phone calls and at events through creative demonstrations and presentations

Preferred skills and interests

  • Ability to take very detailed notes
  • Very creative problem-solving abilities
  • 1-3 years experience working in marketing, business development, PR, or sales or PR
  • Strong organizational, analytical, written and verbal communication skills
  • Experience using Google Apps (Gmail, Calendar, Docs), Dropbox, Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn
  • Interest in writing short-form across social channels, emails and more.
  • Basic HTML/CSS and an interest in learning from and working with programmers
  • Hungry to learn PR, sales, customer service and account management to help make an impact at a growing tech startup.
  • Interested in the public relations industry and journalism
  • Ability to wear multiple hats, jump into projects where needed and ready to do whatever it takes to achieve goals
  • Knowledge of the PR and journalism industries
  • Using social media and having a presence on popular platforms like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn) to help with business promotion with outreach in the right way
  • Sharing lots of animated GIFs
  • Blasting music on a Jambox when no one is around in the office
  • Using the latest and greatest mobile and web apps
