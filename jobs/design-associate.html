Design Associate

We’re looking for a talented design associate to join our team for the spring or summer semester. Our ideal candidate is passionate about what they do, confident in their abilities, and open to learning new things. Sawhorse Media is a technology startup that makes the social web more fun and useful with the Shorty Awards and Muck Rack.

Muck Rack connects journalists with PR pros, companies and sources. Journalists use Muck Rack to build portfolios, track the success of their articles and connect with colleagues. Companies use Muck Rack to get more press and receive real time alerts when they're mentioned in the news.

The Shorty Awards honor the best of social media globally. Past winners include Ricky Gervais, Conan O'Brien, Cory Booker, Grover, Stephen Fry, George Takei and Jerry Seinfeld. The Shortys also honor the best work by brands and media companies on social media. Every year millions of people participate in the nomination process. The annual ceremony brings together people IRL from around the world and generates a lot of hashtags.

Some of the things you may be working on include but are not limited to:

  • Social media and marketing materials
  • Designing landing pages, sales decks, etc based off of our current design assets
  • Adding new pages to our sites
  • Assist in user interface designs
  • Working on HTML emails
  • Help squash bugs
  • Editorial related designs (infographics, charts, etc)
  • QA testing new features
  • Rebranding and redesigning blogs

You don’t have to be good at all of the following things, but you should be great at at least one!

  • Web Design
  • Front-end Web Development (HTML, Sass, Javascript)
  • Back-end Web Development (Django, Python)
  • Visual and Graphic Design
  • UI Design
  • UX (research, ideation, etc)

How to apply

Send us an email at jobs@sawhorsemedia.com and:

  • Include links that demonstrate your work, which could include but are not limited to Github profile, dribbble, portfolio site, sites you’ve created or worked on, or any other projects you’ve completed
  • Tell us why you’re interested in working at Sawhorse
