VP Engineering - NYC

Muck Rack is looking for an ambitious individual to join one of the fastest growing tech startups in NYC. Muck Rack's mission is to make journalists, PR pros and marketers more successful by providing the technology to do so. This includes helping media professionals find journalists to pitch and monitor on a day to day basis via our pro service.

Responsibilities

  • Recruit strong engineers, and refine our hiring processes
  • Provide mentorship, coaching and technical guidance
  • Build and scale our software engineering process
  • Ability to work on code, with significant experience managing complex Django/Python sites in production

Requirements

  • Significant experience running a complex, high-traffic site in production, particularly at a startup or SaaS company
  • 3+ years experience managing and mentoring engineering teams, running software projects, and continuously shipping product
  • Experience recruiting team members
  • Track record of managing successful technical projects a startups or SaaS companies
  • Past hands-on experience at writing quality code
  • Flexibility, great problem-solving skills, and sense of humor

Preferred

  • Interested in journalism, news, media or social media.
  • Experience as a startup founder or CTO
  • Experience with our tech stack and tools: Python, Django, Celery, SASS, Redis, Memcached, Elasticsearch, Nginx, Gunicorn, Varnish, Ansible

How to apply

First, create your profile on Muck Rack. Second, send us an email at jobs@sawhorsemedia.com and:

  • Include links that demonstrate your work, such as your Github profile, personal site, or live projects you’ve built or worked on
  • Tell us why you’re interested in working at Sawhorse
  • Attachments should be PDF, not MS Word.
